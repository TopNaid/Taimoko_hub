Why this platform matters

50% of children in Northern Nigeria lack access to basic education.

Kaso 50% na yara a Arewacin Najeriya ba su da damar samun ilimi na asali.

Approximately 8.4 million girls are out of school in Nigeria due to financial and cultural barriers.

**Kimanin 'yan mata miliyan 8.4 ba sa zuwa makaranta a Najeriya saboda matsalolin kudi da al'adu.**

Over 50% of children lack a dedicated platform like Taimako Hub to effectively engage with and learn.

Fiye da kashi 50% na yara ba su da wata takamaiman kafa kamar Taimako Hub da za su yi amfani da ita wajen koyo da fahimta sosai.
